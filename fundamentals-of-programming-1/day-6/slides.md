# Approximation

---

## Definition of fractions

In the base 10 system, what does the decimal number $0.abc$ mean

$$
0*10^0 + a*10^{-1} + b*10^{-2} + c*10^{-3}
$$

And in binary, it means the same thing

$$
0*2^0 + a*2^{-1} + b*2^{-2} + c*2^{-3}
$$
